Dental Care & Cleanings

Good dental health is essential to your pet’s overall well-being. Just like humans, pets can develop dental issues such as plaque buildup, gum disease, and tooth decay, which can lead to pain, infection, and other serious health problems. Our Dental Care & Cleanings service helps ensure your pet’s mouth stays healthy and clean, preventing long-term dental issues and promoting better overall health.

What’s Included in the Service

  1. Comprehensive Dental Exam
    • A thorough inspection of your pet’s teeth and gums to identify signs of plaque, tartar, gingivitis, or other dental issues.
    • Evaluation of the mouth for signs of infection, loose teeth, or any abnormalities in bite alignment.
  2. Professional Teeth Cleaning
    • Removal of plaque and tartar using specialized tools, including ultrasonic devices to gently clean each tooth.
    • Scaling to prevent the buildup of hard tartar, which can lead to gum disease and other oral health issues.
    • Polishing to smooth out the surface of the teeth, helping prevent future plaque accumulation.
  3. Fluoride Treatment (Optional)
    • An application of fluoride to strengthen your pet's teeth and help reduce sensitivity. This can be particularly beneficial for older pets or those with weaker enamel.
  4. Gum Health Assessment
    • Monitoring the health of your pet’s gums to check for signs of gum disease (gingivitis or periodontal disease).
    • Treatment recommendations if early stages of gum disease are detected.

While every pet’s dental needs are different, most pets should have a professional dental cleaning at least once a year, especially if they have a history of dental problems. Some pets may require more frequent cleanings based on age, breed, and overall oral health.

Regular dental care is an essential part of your pet’s overall health, and our team is here to make sure your pet’s smile stays bright and healthy. Contact us today to schedule a dental exam or cleaning for your pet!
